Breach Counsel
Support

When your client has experienced a breach, you need a technical partner who understands both the cybersecurity landscape and the legal framework. Our practice provides privileged forensic analysis, expert witness services, and defensible documentation designed for the courtroom.

Technical Expertise That Withstands Scrutiny

Breach litigation hinges on the quality and defensibility of the technical investigation. Was the breach preventable? Were reasonable security measures in place? What was the scope of data exposure? These questions require a cybersecurity expert who can investigate, document, and testify with clarity and credibility.

We work directly with breach counsel attorneys — under privilege — to provide the forensic analysis and expert insight that strengthens legal positions. Our work product is designed from inception to be defensible, clear, and persuasive to judges and juries who are not cybersecurity experts.

Capabilities

  • Privileged digital forensic investigation under attorney work product doctrine
  • Expert witness testimony — deposition and trial
  • Forensic reports prepared for legal proceedings, not solely technical audiences
  • Breach causation analysis — timeline, methodology, and contributing factors
  • Reasonableness assessments — evaluation of industry-standard controls
  • Regulatory notification guidance across state breach laws, HIPAA, and PCI
  • Data exposure quantification and impact assessment
  • Technical review of opposing expert reports
  • Remediation oversight to demonstrate good-faith security improvements

For Breach Counsel Attorneys

If you represent clients who have experienced a cybersecurity incident — whether defending against regulatory action, managing class action exposure, or pursuing claims against negligent security providers — our team can serve as your technical expert throughout the engagement.

We understand the sensitivity of these matters. Every engagement begins with a mutual NDA, and all investigation work is conducted under privilege. Communication remains direct and confidential throughout.

For Organizations Facing Litigation

If your organization has experienced a breach and faces legal exposure, it is critical that the forensic investigation be conducted properly from the outset. Evidence preserved incorrectly or findings documented carelessly can undermine your legal position. We ensure the technical dimension of your response supports — rather than jeopardizes — your legal strategy.

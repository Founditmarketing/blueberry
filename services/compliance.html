Compliance &
Risk Assessment

Compliance shouldn't be a checkbox exercise. I help organizations prepare for audits, close security gaps, and build programs that satisfy frameworks while actually improving their security.

Pass the Audit. Fix the Problems.

Most compliance consultants hand you a spreadsheet of findings and walk away. I stay until the work is done — helping you understand where you're exposed, what to prioritize, and how to build sustainable security processes that satisfy auditors and actually protect your business.

Whether you're preparing for your first SOC 2 audit, maintaining ISO 27001 certification, or trying to figure out which of the fifteen frameworks you're supposed to follow — I cut through the complexity and give you a clear path forward.

What I Do

  • Security risk assessments and gap analysis
  • SOC 2 Type I and Type II readiness preparation
  • ISO 27001 implementation and certification support
  • HIPAA security rule compliance assessments
  • FedRAMP authorization support
  • Policy and procedure development
  • Security control implementation and documentation
  • Third-party vendor risk assessments
  • Board-level risk reporting and executive briefings

Who This Is For

Companies preparing for their first compliance audit. Organizations that failed an audit and need help remediating. Businesses whose clients are demanding SOC 2 or ISO 27001 certification. Healthcare providers navigating HIPAA requirements. Anyone who needs to demonstrate security maturity to customers, partners, or regulators.

Detection
Engineering

Detection rules are the core of any security operations program. Our practice develops, tests, and maintains custom detection logic informed by real adversary behavior — ensuring your SOC detects the threats that target your industry.

Threat-Informed Detection That Works

Effective detection engineering requires understanding both the adversary and the environment. Generic vendor-provided rules generate noise. Our approach maps your unique attack surface to the specific threat actors and techniques relevant to your industry, then builds detection logic that reliably identifies malicious activity while minimizing false positives.

Capabilities

  • Custom detection rule development across SIEM, EDR, and cloud platforms
  • MITRE ATT&CK coverage mapping and gap analysis
  • Detection-as-code pipelines for version-controlled rule management
  • Adversary emulation and purple team validation of detection coverage
  • Sigma rule development for cross-platform detection portability
  • False positive analysis and rule tuning optimization
  • Threat-informed prioritization based on industry-specific adversary intelligence
  • Detection maturity assessment and roadmap development
  • Analyst runbook creation for each detection rule

Who We Serve

Security teams seeking to mature their detection capabilities beyond vendor defaults. Organizations building or restructuring security operations programs. SOC managers responsible for demonstrating detection coverage to leadership. Companies that need detection engineering expertise but cannot justify a full-time hire.

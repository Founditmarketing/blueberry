Endpoint Detection

Endpoint detection and response platforms are the frontline of modern security operations. Our practice deploys, configures, and monitors EDR solutions — ensuring endpoint protection delivers detection efficacy rather than alert overload.

Maximizing Endpoint Protection

EDR technology has transformed endpoint security from signature-based prevention to behavioral detection and real-time response. However, the effectiveness of any EDR deployment depends entirely on its configuration, tuning, and the competence of the analysts monitoring it.

Our EDR management service ensures your endpoint protection platform is properly deployed across your entire environment, configured for your specific threat landscape, continuously tuned for optimal detection, and monitored by experienced analysts capable of distinguishing genuine threats from benign anomalies.

Capabilities

  • EDR platform deployment and configuration optimization
  • Cross-platform management (CrowdStrike, SentinelOne, Defender, Carbon Black)
  • Custom detection policy development for your environment
  • Behavioral detection tuning and false positive reduction
  • Real-time alert monitoring and triage by experienced analysts
  • Automated and manual threat containment and isolation
  • Threat hunting across endpoint telemetry
  • EDR health monitoring and agent deployment compliance
  • Monthly detection efficacy reporting

Who We Serve

Organizations seeking expert management of their EDR investment. Companies deploying EDR for the first time and needing configuration guidance. Security teams overwhelmed by EDR alert volume. Organizations requiring 24/7 monitoring of endpoint detection data.

  • VMware Carbon Black

Personal Security

Executive
Cybersecurity Protection

C-suite executives are high-value targets for cybercriminals seeking financial fraud, corporate espionage, and reputational damage. Our practice provides comprehensive personal cybersecurity tailored to each executive’s unique risk profile.

Protecting the Principal

Executives present unique attack surfaces that enterprise security programs do not address. Personal devices, family accounts, social media profiles, home networks, and travel patterns all create opportunities for sophisticated threat actors. The consequences of a successful attack against an executive extend beyond the individual to the organization they lead.

We deliver discreet, comprehensive personal cybersecurity assessments and ongoing monitoring services. Each engagement begins with a thorough evaluation of the executive’s digital footprint, personal account security, device posture, and family exposure — resulting in a prioritized protection plan tailored to their specific risk profile.

Capabilities

  • Personal device audit and hardening across all platforms
  • Account security review — email, financial, cloud, social media
  • Home network security assessment and configuration
  • Personal data exposure assessment and data broker removal
  • Social media privacy audit and configuration
  • Travel security preparation and temporary device provisioning
  • Family member digital protection setup
  • Ongoing personal threat monitoring and alerting
  • VIP incident response for personal account compromise

Who We Serve

C-suite executives and board members of publicly traded companies. High-net-worth individuals and family offices. Public figures and individuals with high-visibility profiles. Executive teams at organizations in targeted industries.

Protection Areas

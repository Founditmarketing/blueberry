Executive & Personal
Security

High-profile individuals face targeted cyber threats that standard enterprise security doesn't cover. I provide discreet, comprehensive personal cybersecurity for executives, public figures, and their families.

Your Digital Life, Protected

If you're a CEO, board member, public figure, or high-net-worth individual, your personal devices, accounts, and online presence are attack surfaces. Threat actors target executives for financial fraud, corporate espionage, and reputational damage — and most personal security measures are woefully inadequate.

I provide the same caliber of protection that Fortune 50 executives receive — tailored to your specific risk profile, delivered with complete discretion.

What I Do

  • Personal device security audit and hardening (phones, laptops, tablets)
  • Account security review — email, banking, social media, cloud storage
  • Stalkerware and spyware detection and removal
  • Dark web monitoring for personal data exposure
  • Travel security briefings and temporary device provisioning
  • Family member security awareness and protection setup
  • Social engineering defense training
  • Privacy management — data broker removal, digital footprint reduction
  • Incident response for personal account compromise

Who This Is For

C-suite executives concerned about their personal digital exposure. High-net-worth individuals and families. Public figures, influencers, and anyone with a high-visibility profile. Individuals going through high-stakes legal proceedings, divorce, or custody disputes who suspect digital surveillance.

  • Dark Web Monitoring
  • Privacy Management
  • Travel Security
  • Family Protection

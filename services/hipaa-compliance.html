Healthcare Compliance

HIPAA Security
Compliance

Healthcare organizations face unique cybersecurity challenges — protecting patient data while maintaining clinical workflows. Our practice helps covered entities and business associates achieve and maintain HIPAA Security Rule compliance with practical, operationally sustainable security programs.

Protecting Patient Data

The healthcare sector is consistently the most targeted industry for data breaches, and HIPAA enforcement actions carry penalties ranging from $100 to $50,000 per violation, with annual maximums of $1.5 million per violation category. Beyond regulatory penalties, healthcare breaches fundamentally damage patient trust and organizational reputation.

Our HIPAA practice addresses the complete spectrum of Security Rule requirements — administrative, physical, and technical safeguards — with a focus on implementing controls that protect patient data without impeding clinical operations. We understand that security in healthcare must accommodate clinical workflows, not obstruct them.

Capabilities

  • HIPAA Security Rule risk analysis and risk management
  • Administrative safeguard assessment and policy development
  • Physical safeguard evaluation for facilities and equipment
  • Technical safeguard implementation — access controls, encryption, audit logs
  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA) review and compliance verification
  • Breach notification procedure development and testing
  • Security awareness training tailored to healthcare staff
  • EHR and medical device security assessment
  • OCR audit preparation and response support

Who We Serve

Hospitals, health systems, and physician practices managing ePHI. Health IT companies and SaaS providers serving as Business Associates. Healthcare organizations that have experienced a breach or received an OCR inquiry. Covered entities seeking to proactively strengthen their HIPAA compliance posture.

Incident Response &
Digital Forensics

You've been breached. Time matters. I provide rapid, hands-on response — from containment to recovery — with forensic documentation that holds up under legal and regulatory scrutiny.

When Every Hour Counts

A security incident doesn't wait for business hours. Whether it's ransomware locking down your operations, a business email compromise draining accounts, or a nation-state actor inside your network — I'm available around the clock to lead your response.

I've handled incidents at Fortune 50 scale and for small businesses discovering their first breach. The process is the same: contain the threat, preserve evidence, eradicate the attacker, and restore operations — in that order.

What I Do

  • Ransomware containment, negotiation guidance, and recovery
  • Business email compromise (BEC) investigation and remediation
  • Cloud intrusion response (AWS, Azure, GCP, M365)
  • Advanced persistent threat (APT) detection and eviction
  • Forensic disk, memory, and network analysis
  • Malware reverse engineering and indicator extraction
  • Evidence preservation with defensible chain of custody
  • Regulatory notification guidance (HIPAA, PCI, state breach laws)
  • Post-incident security hardening recommendations

Who This Is For

Business owners who've been hacked and need someone they can trust to take control of the situation. Attorneys representing breached clients who need privileged forensic analysis. Enterprises that need a senior IR lead — not a junior analyst reading a playbook.

How It Works

Call or submit an inquiry. I respond within hours — often minutes for active incidents. We establish scope, execute containment, and I stay engaged through full remediation. Every engagement includes a detailed forensic report suitable for legal, insurance, and regulatory purposes.

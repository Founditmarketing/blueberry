Managed Detection
& Response

Not every organization requires a full in-house SOC. We partner with businesses to provide continuous monitoring, proactive threat hunting, and rapid incident escalation — delivering enterprise-grade protection without the overhead of building it internally.

Enterprise-Grade Protection, Right-Sized

Organizations should not need a seven-figure security budget to achieve meaningful threat detection. We connect clients with the right combination of technology, monitoring, and expert human analysis to provide continuous protection tailored to their environment and risk profile.

Unlike commodity MDR providers that rely on automated playbooks for every alert, our approach prioritizes senior human analysis. When an alert triggers, an experienced analyst evaluates it — and when the situation demands it, our principal consultant is personally engaged in the response.

Capabilities

  • 24/7 security monitoring and alerting
  • Proactive threat hunting across client environments
  • Endpoint detection and response (EDR) management
  • Cloud security monitoring across AWS, Azure, Microsoft 365, and GCP
  • Log management, correlation, and analysis
  • Incident escalation and initial response triage
  • Monthly threat reports and security posture reviews
  • Ongoing detection rule tuning and coverage improvement

Who We Serve

Mid-market organizations that require security monitoring but cannot staff a full SOC team. Business owners who understand their exposure but require guidance on where to begin. Organizations that experienced inadequate results from prior MSSP engagements. Any organization that values experienced human oversight over purely automated detection.

Ransomware Response
& Recovery

Your systems are encrypted. Operations are halted. Every hour costs revenue, trust, and regulatory exposure. Our practice provides immediate, senior-led ransomware response — from containment and negotiation advisory through full recovery and forensic documentation.

Immediate Containment. Structured Recovery.

Ransomware attacks are among the most disruptive cybersecurity events an organization can face. Whether a single file server or an entire enterprise domain has been compromised, the response methodology remains the same: isolate the threat, preserve forensic evidence, assess recovery options, and restore operations — executed with the speed and precision that the situation demands.

Our team has managed ransomware incidents across Fortune 50 enterprises and mid-market organizations. We understand the intersection of technical response, legal obligation, insurance coordination, and business continuity that defines these engagements.

Our Ransomware Response Process

  • Immediate threat containment and lateral movement prevention
  • Ransomware variant identification and attacker attribution
  • Negotiation advisory — evaluating payment decisions with legal counsel
  • Decryption feasibility assessment and key recovery
  • Backup integrity validation and prioritized restoration
  • Forensic root cause analysis — how the attacker gained access
  • Data exfiltration assessment — determining if data was stolen before encryption
  • Regulatory notification guidance across applicable breach laws
  • Insurance carrier coordination and claims documentation
  • Post-incident security hardening to prevent reinfection

Why Response Speed Matters

Every hour of downtime during a ransomware event translates directly to lost revenue, damaged client relationships, and increased regulatory scrutiny. Organizations that engage experienced incident responders within the first hours of detection consistently achieve faster containment, reduced data loss, and stronger post-incident legal positioning.

Who We Serve

Organizations facing an active ransomware attack who need immediate expert assistance. Breach counsel attorneys coordinating privileged incident response. Insurance carriers and adjusters managing policyholder claims. IT teams that have contained the initial threat but require forensic investigation and recovery support.

After the Crisis

Recovery extends beyond restoring systems. Every ransomware engagement concludes with a detailed forensic report documenting the attack timeline, attacker methodology, and specific security improvements to prevent recurrence. This documentation serves legal, insurance, and regulatory requirements while providing a clear remediation roadmap.

You focus on your clients. We deliver world-class cybersecurity. When you refer qualified organizations to Varcoe Cybersecurity, you earn ongoing revenue while your clients receive Fortune 50-caliber security expertise.

A Partnership Built on Trust & Results

Our referral partnership program is designed for professionals who regularly encounter cybersecurity needs in their client base — but don't want to build a security practice from scratch. You bring the relationship. We bring the operational expertise. Your clients get protected. You get paid.

Every referred engagement is handled with the same discretion, urgency, and technical depth that Fortune 50 enterprises expect. Your reputation is our reputation.

How It Works

  • Submit a qualified referral through our partner portal or direct introduction
  • We conduct a confidential consultation with the referred organization
  • Upon engagement, you receive an ongoing revenue share for the life of the relationship
  • Quarterly partner reporting and transparent deal tracking
  • Co-branded materials available for qualified partners

Ideal Partner Profiles

  • Managed Service Providers (MSPs) without dedicated security practice
  • IT consulting firms encountering compliance or breach response needs
  • Attorneys specializing in business, data privacy, or breach litigation
  • Insurance brokers writing cyber liability policies
  • Compliance consultants working with regulated industries
  • Accounting firms advising on risk management
  • Private equity firms with portfolio company security needs

Partner Benefits

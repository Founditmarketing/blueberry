A SIEM platform is only as effective as its configuration. Our practice optimizes existing SIEM deployments and architects new implementations — ensuring your investment delivers actionable detection rather than noise.

Maximize Your SIEM Investment

Organizations routinely invest six or seven figures in SIEM technology only to find their implementation generates thousands of daily alerts — the vast majority false positives — while genuine threats pass undetected. The problem is rarely the technology. It is the architecture, log source coverage, detection logic, and operational workflow built around it.

Our SIEM practice addresses these challenges directly. We assess your current deployment, identify coverage gaps, optimize detection rules, reduce false positive volume, and establish sustainable operational processes that your analyst team can maintain independently.

Capabilities

  • SIEM platform assessment and optimization roadmap
  • New SIEM deployment architecture and implementation
  • Log source onboarding and normalization across infrastructure
  • Detection rule development, testing, and tuning
  • False positive reduction and alert prioritization
  • SIEM-to-SOAR integration and automated response playbooks
  • Threat-informed detection aligned to MITRE ATT&CK coverage gaps
  • Cost optimization — data ingestion reduction without losing visibility
  • SIEM migration planning and execution between platforms

Platforms We Support

Our team has deep operational experience across all major SIEM platforms: Splunk Enterprise and Cloud, Microsoft Sentinel, Elastic Security, Google Chronicle, IBM QRadar, and LogRhythm. Our guidance is vendor-agnostic — we recommend the platform that fits your requirements, not the one that generates the largest referral fee.

Who We Serve

CISOs seeking to demonstrate detection coverage improvements to leadership. Companies that want to reduce SIEM licensing costs without sacrificing security visibility.

Services

Executive-level cybersecurity leadership without the $400K+ salary. Our vCISO program provides strategic security guidance, board-level reporting, and hands-on program management — delivered by practitioners with Fortune 50 pedigree.

Enterprise Security Leadership, On Demand

Most organizations need a CISO but can't justify the cost of a full-time executive hire. Our virtual CISO service delivers the strategic vision, operational oversight, and regulatory expertise of a seasoned security leader — at a fraction of the cost.

Unlike consultants who deliver reports and disappear, your vCISO becomes an embedded member of your leadership team. We attend board meetings, manage vendor relationships, oversee security programs, and drive measurable improvements in your security posture.

vCISO Responsibilities

  • Security strategy development and roadmap creation
  • Board and executive committee security briefings
  • Security program development and maturity assessment
  • Vendor and tool evaluation, selection, and management
  • Compliance program oversight (CMMC, NIST, SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001)
  • Incident response planning and tabletop exercises
  • Security budget development and ROI justification
  • Policy and procedure development
  • Third-party risk management program
  • Security awareness program design

Who Benefits from vCISO Services

  • Mid-market companies ($10M-$500M revenue) without a dedicated CISO
  • Defense contractors requiring CMMC compliance leadership
  • Healthcare organizations navigating HIPAA security requirements
  • SaaS companies pursuing SOC 2 certification
  • Organizations in transition — post-breach, post-acquisition, or rapid growth
  • Companies with a CISO vacancy during executive search
